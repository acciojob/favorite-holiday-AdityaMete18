Goa


Goa is one of India's most popular tourist destinations, known for its beautiful beaches, Portuguese heritage, vibrant nightlife, and delicious seafood. It offers a perfect mix of relaxation, adventure, and cultural experiences.

  1. Baga Beach
  2. Calangute Beach
  3. Basilica of Bom Jesus
  4. Dudhsagar Waterfalls
  5. Fort Aguada
  • Goan Fish Curry
  • Chicken Xacuti
  • Prawn Balchão
  • Pork Vindaloo
  • Bebinca (Traditional Goan dessert)
